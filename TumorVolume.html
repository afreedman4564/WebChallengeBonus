Tumor Volume






Evaluating tumor volume for outliers, one was uncovered across a four regimen review that includes: Infubinol, Ceftamin, Ramicane, and Capomulin. While the outlier shows a tumor size consistent with better performing regimens, the opinion of the impacted regimen (Infubinol) is unchanged so strong reason to remove the outlier is not necessary.
